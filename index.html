RETF2

In July 2014, I was a member of the crew Bad Wolf team who organized the Retrospective event at the Euskal Encounter 22 LAN party in Bilbao, Spain.


With two other friends, we designed, modeled, textured, and rigged two medals for the Team Fortress 2 video game, one as a winner prize and other for the participants. Both of them were added to the game by VALVE.

At 2015 we did it again, this time with support from eXtelevision. The Retrospective 2 tournament had over 2300 participants in a total of 5 formats (Highlander, 6v6, RSP, Arena respawn, and Ultiduo).

OnlineSDK

Name shortened from Spanish Development Kit, it was a spanish project, made from scratch, focused on teaching or expand their knowledge in the creation and modification on content related to the game engine of Half-Life 2, the Source engine.


It was born as a site where I could share my documentation about modding and a way for learn HTML, CSS, and PHP on my way.


Right now it's only an abandoned project.
